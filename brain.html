00 · setup — the abyss

Begin below the light

The deepest water on the whole journey. One faint promise burns far overhead; everything else is silhouette and falling snow. This is where the curriculum starts — in the dark, before the first tool is even named.

01 · python · git · swe — the ascent

Rise toward the surface

The promise becomes a milky disc of daylight. Color returns in physical order — blue, then teal, then green — as the water thins. The first real work happens here, on the way up.

02 · mathematics — the cathedral

Stand inside the light

Sunlight folded by the surface into a living net of caustics, rays leaning through the blue. Mathematics is this building: light obeying rules, and the rules made visible.

03 · cs foundations — the shallows

Where the light lands

The cathedral's caustics finally reach the ground and break across the sand. Algorithms, data structures, the machine itself — solid footing under clear water, the first place on the journey where you can stand.

04 · classical ml — the surface

Learn to read the sea

Regression, trees, clustering — the old instruments. Up here the water is all signal: every crest a distribution, every fleck of glitter a residual. Learn what the sea is saying before you ask it to speak.

05 · deep learning — the river

Follow the gradient down

Water never computes the slope; it simply descends. Backpropagation is this river — and braided beneath the bright channel lie the ghosts of every path it tried before. The river remembers itself.

06 · transformers — the confluence

Two waters meet

Attention is a meeting of currents: every token weighing every other, and refusing to blur. The dark water and the jade water braid along one live seam — the architecture this whole journey is named for.

07 · evals & post-training — the estuary

Measured against the sea

The river ends where it is tested. Twice a day the tide climbs the flats and grades every channel the water carved. Benchmarks, alignment, preference — here fresh water learns what the ocean wants.

08 · agents — the rain

Water leaves the river

Until now the water only flowed. Here it climbs into the sky and goes out to act on the world — tools, plans, consequences. And it arrives the way rain always does: on the glass, an inch from your face.

09 · systems & gpus — the roots

The buried network

Under every forest runs a web that trades light for sugar. Kernels, memory, interconnects — the cold infrastructure pulsing beneath everything you have built so far, wavelength-coded and quietly alive.

10 · specialization — the canopy

Choose your tree

The forest the roots were feeding. Sunlight falls through a thousand possible crowns — vision, language, biology, control — and you climb only one. The light that reaches you is shaped by the tree you chose.

11 · replication — the garden at dusk

Tend what others planted

Blue hour. You take a published result and grow it again from seed. Every reproduced finding is a firefly — one small verified light, answering the others across the dark garden.

12 · original research — the aurora

The ceiling of the world

Above the garden the sky itself is alive. This is work no one has done yet — charged, rare, visible for miles. The journey ends where the water began: as light, returned to the sky.
